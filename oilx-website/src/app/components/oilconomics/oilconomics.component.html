OILX Tokenomics

OILX has a total supply of 1 billion tokens. The distribution is as follows:

  • 30%: Reserved for community rewards and staking.
  • 20%: Allocated for development and team incentives.
  • 25%: Held for future partnerships and strategic reserves.
  • 15%: Initial exchange offerings and liquidity pools.
  • 10%: Marketing and user acquisition campaigns.
